## Notes for Selection

**Consider:**
- Broader appeal vs. niche interest
- AI's ability to generate quality questions in that domain
- Balance between "fun" and "educational"
- Avoiding categories that are too similar (prevents dilution)
- Regional diversity and cultural representation
- Mix of timeless vs. contemporary topics

**Recommendation:**
Start with **60 total categories** (add 40 from this list) for a perfect 2-month daily rotation cycle. Can always expand to 90 for a 3-month cycle later.
